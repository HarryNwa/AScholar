ABOUT AScholar

AScholar Education Services provides admissions services to global aspiring students beginning from Nigeria. On our STAS platform, we conduct series of online tests spanning through five (5) stages including quick comprehension passages, audio listening, speaking skills and writing to examine our applicants' academic potentials.

The online test is the first of two phases organised by AScholar. The second phase is a reality TV show which will give successful applicants from the first phase the opportunity to prove their academic potential and showcase their general talents.

All applicants are hereby required to study the syllabus available on the Class Room page and take our free online document, audio and video lectures.

You are also expected to read your copy of literature downloaded upon registering for AScholar test. It will be used for your Use of English.
