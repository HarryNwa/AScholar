The AScholar Test

The test contains 25 questions and there is a 30 second time limit.

The test is an official way to see how much you know, or don't know, about your course of interest.

Count Your Score

You will get 1 point for each correct answer. At the end of the Quiz, your total score will be displayed on your Active Score and General Progression tables respectively. Maximum score is 25 points.

Good Luck!
